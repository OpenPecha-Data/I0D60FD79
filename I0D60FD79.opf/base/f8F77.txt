caturbhyo ’nye tu saṃskāraskandhaḥ

rūpavedanāsaṃjñāvijñānebhyaś caturbhyo ’nye tu saṃskārāḥ saṃskāraskandhaḥ |bhagavatā tu sūtre ṣaṭ cetanākāyā ity uktaṃ pr
ādhānyāt |sā hi karmasvarūpatvād abhisaṃskaraṇe pradhānā |ata evoktaṃ bhagavatā saṃskṛtam abhisaṃskaroti |tasmāt saṃskār
ā upādānaskandha ity ucyata itianyathā hi śeṣāṇāṃ caitasikānāṃ viprayuktānāṃ ca saṃskārāṇāṃ skandhāsaṃgrahād duḥkhasamud
ayasatyatvaṃ na syād iti parijñāparihāṇe api na syātām |uktaṃ ca bhagavatā nāham ekadharmam api anabhijñāyāparijñāya duḥ
khasyāntakriyāṃ vadāmīti |evam aprahāyety uktam |tasmād avaśyam eṣāṃ saṃskāraskandhasaṃgraho ’bhyupagantavyaḥ |

ete punas trayaḥ |

vedanāsaṃjñāsaṃskāraskandhāḥ āyatanadhātuvyavasthāyāṃ

dharmāyatanadhātvākhyāḥ sahāvijñapty asaṃskṛtaiḥ || 1.15 ||

ity etāni sapta dravyāṇi dharmāyatanaṃ dharmadhātuś cety ākhyāyante |
