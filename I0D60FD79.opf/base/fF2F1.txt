pañca rūpādayaḥ skandhā ity uktam | tatra

rūpaṃ pañcendriyāṇy arthāḥ pañcāvijñaptir eva ca |

pañcendriyāṇi cakṣuḥśrotraghrāṇajihvākāyendriyāṇi |pañcārthās teṣām eva cakṣurādīnām indriyāṇāṃ yathāsvaṃ ye pañca viṣayā
ḥ rūpaśabdagandharasaspraṣṭavyākhyāḥ |avijñaptiś ceti |etāvān rūpaskandhaḥtatra ya ete pañca rūpādayo ’rthā uktāḥ |

tadvijñānāśrayā rūpaprasādāś cakṣurādayaḥ || 1.9 ||

rūpaśabdagandharasaspraṣṭavyavijñānānām āśrayabhūtā ye pañca rūpātmakāḥ prasādās te yathā kramaṃ cakṣuḥśrotraghrāṇajihvāk
āyā veditavyāḥ |yathoktaṃ bhagavatā cakṣur bhikṣo ādhyātmikam āyatanaṃ catvāri mahābhutāny upādāya iti vistaraḥ |yāny et
āni cakṣurādīny uktāni tadvijñānāśrayā rūpaprasādāś cakṣurādayaḥ |cakṣurvijñānādyāśrayā ity arthaḥ |evaṃ kṛtvā prakaraṇa
graṇtho ’py anuvṛtto bhavati |
